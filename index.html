About UrFU

Ural Federal University (UrFU) is a modern and dynamic university located in Yekaterinburg, Russia. Established in 1920, UrFU is one of the leading universities in Russia and is recognized for its high-quality education and research in various fields.

Programs

  • Engineering

    Study various engineering disciplines, including mechanical, electrical, and chemical engineering, and gain practical experience in state-of-the-art laboratories.

  • Business Administration

    Develop your leadership and management skills and learn about various aspects of business administration, including finance, marketing, and human resources.
